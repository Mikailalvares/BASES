The hallmark of Balambala Secondary School's success has catapulted it into the national spotlight. The school's educational philosophy is firmly rooted in the concept of holistic education, addressing the spiritual, social, physical, and intellectual dimensions of students' development. This comprehensive approach is vividly demonstrated through the remarkable results consistently achieved by the school in the Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education (KCSE) examinations over the years.

About our School

Balambala Secondary School is an esteemed educational institution located in the heart of Balambala, Garissa County, Kenya. With a rich history spanning several decades, the school has earned a well-deserved reputation for its commitment to academic excellence and holistic development.
