School History

Established in 1992, BALAMBALA SECONDARY SCHOOL has a remarkable story that echoes through time. Born in the heart of North Eastern, our school quickly became a legend, earning the revered title of "The Giant in the Bush."

With each passing year, our institution's legacy grew stronger, captivating not only the region but the entire nation. Our journey has been one of continuous growth, unwavering determination, and an unquenchable thirst for knowledge.

Today, we stand proud as an educational beacon, lighting the path to excellence for generations to come. Our history is a testament to the relentless pursuit of greatness, and we invite you to be a part of our enduring legacy.
